O-S-V VALUES & BELIEFS

INCLUSION

It is very important for owners Line and Andrea to create a comfortable environment so everybody feels welcomed and included. This is shown by an equal focus on women- and men's wear and by O-S-V opening a café in store in September 2023 so customers can stay as long as they want and grab a coffee to stay or to go. 

JOY

Our intention is to facilitate a joyful space for both our team and our customers. It is essential to us that both parts feel appreciated and benefit from our work.  

CIRCULARITY

Creating a circular and conscious work environment. The food we eat, the paint we use, the atmosphere we create together are part of the things we keep sustainable and healthy. O-S-V is short for "og så videre" which translates to "and so on”. It represents our vision of giving old clothes new life and to lessen waste in the world.

TRUST

We carefully curate the store with clothes from private customers. The clothes are well taken care of the whole O-S-V team, and the money is transferred every Monday by our admin. We are so lucky to have loyal customers who buy and sell their clothes in our store, so we aim to make it a smooth and trustworthy experience for all parties. 

TEAMWORK

Our green and sustainable values are also the reason for our POP UP on the first Saturday of each month. We have POP UP events in O-S-V in collaboration with various local and sustainable businesses. We find it very important to support the sustainable initiatives around us by sharing our lovely location and space.

O-S-V is a carefully curated secondhand store located in central Copenhagen.
